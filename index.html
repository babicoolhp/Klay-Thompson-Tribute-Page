KLAY THOMPSON

About Klay Thompson

  • Klay Alexander Thompson (born February 8, 1990) is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

  • Thompson is the son of former NBA player Mychal Thompson. He played college basketball for three seasons with the Washington State Cougars, where he was a two-time first-team all-conference selection in the Pac-10 (now Pac-12). He was selected in the first round of the 2011 NBA draft by Golden State with the 11th overall pick.

Personal accomplishments and awards

  • 4× NBA champion: 2015, 2017, 2018, 2022
  • 5× NBA All-Star: 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019
  • 2× All-NBA Third Team: 2015, 2016
  • NBA All-Defensive Second Team: 2019
  • NBA Three-Point Contest champion: 2016
  • NBA All-Rookie First Team: 2012

Shooting form

Klay is famous for a unique quick released shooting form. He has one of the fastest releasing time in the NBA. He is also amongst the most accurate shooting players with 41.7% from the 3-point line.
